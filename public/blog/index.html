The Democrats’ Project 2029 Has To Go Big

Democrats are pitching their version of Project 2025, and apparently it sucks so far. Project 2029 has to be things that are big, simple, exciting, and memorable. It’s got to be full of monumental swings. Not things that “everyone agrees on;” things that get people talking:

  1. Ban gerrymandering
  2. Public option by executive order
  3. IRS fills out your taxes for you
  4. Rip up NEPA to build housing and transportation
  5. Break up DHS
  6. End Citizens United
